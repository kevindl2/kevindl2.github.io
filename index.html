About Me

Biography

I am a 1st year MCS student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I graduated from U of I with a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering. My previous research experience is in the Nanoscale Manufacturing Node (see Software Projects) at U of I, working on image analysis and data visualization of graphene. I have interned at the manufacturing company K.R. Komarek as a Mechanical Engineering Intern and at the software company MathWorks as a Release Engineering Intern. I previously worked full-time at Sargent and Lundy as an Instrumentation and Controls Engineer.
